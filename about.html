Larobe is?

This is a collection we use to make women proud of themselves, let them know they are resilient and nothing can stop their dreams. This collection is used to empower women towards greater things.LA ROBE is a female owned, carefully curated space for women, and lovers of women to encourage the spread of love, healing and positivity as well as promote self love and self care through the art. La Robe is a community created for women to feel supported, encouraged, loved and appreciated as they navigate through spaces that might make them feel otherwise, equipping them cute clothing, accessories, affirmations, and other tools to do so. La Robe promotes beauty on the inside as well as on the out. Most products are handmade, ethically with love.
